Tsukasa is a short boy with choppy black hair and wide amber eyes. He looks nearly identical to his older twin brother, Hanako.

Tsukasa

As a ghost, Tsukasa appears to be cheerful and extremely childish. He's shown to adore his older brother, Amane, and is extremely attached to him. Due to this childish demeanor, he is often treated like a little kid by Sakura, his assistant, whom he is bound to by contract. Conversely, he is capable of carrying out extreme acts of violence with no remorse.
